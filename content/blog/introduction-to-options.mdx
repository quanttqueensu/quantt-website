Options are financial derivatives that give the holder the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell an underlying asset at a specified price.

## Key Concepts

### Call Options
A call option gives you the right to **buy** an asset at the strike price.

### Put Options
A put option gives you the right to **sell** an asset at the strike price.

## The Greeks
- **Delta** — sensitivity to underlying price
- **Gamma** — rate of change of delta
- **Theta** — time decay
- **Vega** — sensitivity to volatility
